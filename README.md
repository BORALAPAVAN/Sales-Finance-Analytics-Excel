### **1. Data Extraction & Transformation (ETL)**  
**Tool Used:** Power Query (Excel)  
- Imported data from multiple raw sources  
- Cleaned, standardized, and transformed for accuracy  
- Ensured reliability by removing duplicates, fixing date formats, and normalizing values  

**Outcome:** High-quality data foundation for analysis with minimal errors.

---

### **2. Data Modeling**  
**Tool Used:** Power Pivot  
- Established relationships between fact and dimension tables  
- Designed a star-schema-like structure covering Date, Customer, Market, Product, etc.  
- Enabled seamless multi-dimensional analysis  

**Outcome:** A dynamic and flexible data model that strengthened analytical depth.

---

### **3. Sales Analytics**  
**Focus Areas:**  
- **Customer Performance:** Identified top customers and revenue trends  
- **Market vs Target Performance:** Compared actual vs target numbers across regions and segments  

**Outcome:** Generated actionable insights that support sales strategy refinement and performance tracking.

---

### **4. Finance Analytics**  
**Focus Areas:**  
- **P&L Analysis by Market:** Highlighted profitability and cost distribution  
- **Fiscal Year & Fiscal Month Analysis:** Provided time-based financial insights  
- **Profit Drivers:** Identified the factors influencing financial outcomes  

**Outcome:** Clear understanding of financial dynamics to support better budgeting and planning.

---

### **5. Data Visualization & Reporting**  
**Tools Used:** Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts)  
- Designed visually appealing dashboards and reports  
- Focused on usability with clean layouts and intuitive navigation  

**Outcome:** Insightful, easy-to-read reports that effectively communicate business insights.
